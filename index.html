It's more than just a show

For me, Entourage has more than entertainment value. It actually changed my life. You think that's an exaggeration? Well, it's not.

When I was younger, most of my peers did not know how to pronounce my name: Ari. It's a Hebrew name. I like it. But I grew up in an Irish Catholic neighborhood. In little league, I was known as Air-ee. My name really rhymes with 'sorry'.

Then Entourage -- and Ari Gold -- came along.

Ari was a star character. His colorful, vulgar creativity coupled with an occasionally shown vulnerable and sweet side endeared him to viewers. Suddenly, people knew how to pronounce my name properly.

So, thank you to Ari Gold and the man who created him, Doug Ellin.
